## SERP Learnings

- "chaise lounge sydney" (40/mo) — SERP confirmed low competition: Sydney Furniture Factory, Monster Furniture, Known for Lounges, Sydney Furniture Direct. No national chains. National "chaise lounge" (7,600/mo) rejected — Freedom, Amart, Lounge Lovers dominate.
- "recliner chairs sydney" (110/mo) — SERP confirmed low competition: Sydney Furniture Factory, Monster Furniture (×3), Sydney Furniture Direct. Plush is largest brand present — mid-tier, beatable.
- "sofa bed sydney" rejected — medium competition (dedicated specialists + IKEA). Replaced with fabric lounges page.
- "outdoor furniture" keywords — all either over cap (2,400/mo) or pulled clearance/discount SERPs. Outdoor page dropped, replaced with Corner Lounges.
- "for sale" + location — consistently pulls classified (Gumtree, Facebook Marketplace) and discount/clearance SERPs. Do not use.
- "dining chairs" and "dining tables" — separate SERPs with different competitive sets. Cannot map to one page. Dropped from plan (no budget for 2 pages).
- "kitchen furniture" — renovation/cabinet SERP, not furniture retailers.
- "lighting" — designer specialist SERP, wrong competitive set.
- "furniture showroom sydney" (6,600/mo) — high competition, premium/designer showroom SERP. Used as homepage entity-building signal only, not a ranking target.
- "recliner sofas sydney" — Freedom, Harvey Norman, Fantastic Furniture all ranking. Rejected.
- "recliner lounges sydney" (re-validated 8 Apr 2026) — local specialists hold 1–3 (Known for Lounges, Sydney Furniture Factory, Sydney Furniture Direct), Harvey Norman #4, Nick Scali #5, Monster Furniture #6. Kept as secondary per one-result rule — competitive path into top 3 exists.
- "fabric sofa sydney" (re-validated 8 Apr 2026) — Freedom no longer in SERP (was at #8 in v1.3). Now fully clean: Known for Lounges, Santosa, Glicks, Monster Furniture, Sydney Furniture Factory, DJC Furniture.
- "corner lounge sydney" (re-validated 8 Apr 2026) — SERP has improved since v1.3. Amart no longer present. Harvey Norman appears at #9 only. Local specialists dominate positions 1–8.
- "storage bed sydney" (40/mo) — only clean bedroom SERP found, but no valid secondary keyword identified (all bedroom piece secondaries had national chains).
- Bedroom pieces rejected (all had national chains at meaningful volume): king bed frame sydney, queen storage bed, ottoman bed sydney, gas lift bed base, bedside tables sydney, tallboy sydney, chest of drawers sydney, entertainment unit sydney, buffet/sideboard sydney.
- "3 seater sofa sydney" — Freedom + IKEA + Lounge Lovers + King Living. Rejected.
- "coffee tables sydney" (390/mo) — clean SERP: Shack, Glicks, Demir Leather, Wood World, Dellis. No major chains. Selected as 10th page.
- Focus on Furniture competitor: chaise lounge page drives 2,002/mo traffic at KD 0 — validated high-value target.
