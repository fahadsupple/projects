## SERP Key Learnings

1. **"suspended ceilings melbourne"** → Inner service pages rank (e.g., /suspended-ceiling-systems/, /service/suspended-ceilings). Homepages do NOT dominate. Dedicated service page required.
2. **"drop ceilings" / "grid ceilings"** → Same SERP as "suspended ceilings." These are alternate names only. Put all three on the suspended ceilings service page.
3. **"office partitions melbourne"** → Dedicated inner pages with keyword in URL win (/office-partitions-melbourne/). Pattern is consistent across competitors.
4. **"partition walls melbourne"** → Overlapping SERP with "office partitions." Both can coexist on the same page (Ultimate Fitouts ranks for "partition walls" with its /services/office-partitions/ page).
5. **"glass office partitions" / "glass partition walls"** → Separate glass-specific pages rank (/glass-partitions-melbourne/, /glass-partitions/). Distinct service page justified.
6. **"plasterboard partitions" / "metal stud partitions"** → Both are material/method terms. Similar SERP. Can coexist on one page (e.g., /metal-stud-plasterboard-partitions/ or /plasterboard-partitions-melbourne/).
7. **"ceiling repair melbourne" / "ceiling replacement melbourne"** → Share the same SERP. Repair pages absorb replacement intent. One combined page works.
8. **Location pages** → AJ Ceilings (named competitor) uses /areas-we-serve/[suburb]/ with one page per suburb covering all their services. However, with TWO location keyword roots (suspended ceilings + partition walls), keyword-in-URL flat structure (/suspended-ceilings-[suburb]/ and /partition-walls-[suburb]/) is stronger and directly matches keywords.
