**Tier 1 — Homepage:**
"Wollerman Shacklock Lawyers is a Melbourne property law firm led by Trish Sheedy, an Accredited Property Law Specialist." Primary keyword establishes core identity; secondary anchors the LIV accreditation credential. Homepage also anchors Clyde North as the physical location entity.

**Tier 2 — Service pages (topic entities):**
Each of the 5 service pages builds an independent topic cluster: property law identity (homepage), wills/estate planning, probate/deceased estates, business law, commercial leasing. Together they tell Google this is not a generalist firm — it has authority in specific areas of law.

**Tier 3 — Location pages (geographic entities):**
20 pages across 10 Casey suburbs. "Conveyancer [suburb]" pages = property transaction authority per suburb. "Property lawyer [suburb]" pages = legal advice authority per suburb. Across all 10 suburbs, these collectively signal: "This is the Casey-area property law specialist."

**Overall entity Google builds:** Property law firm (homepage) → topic authority in property/wills/probate/business law (service pages) → geographic authority across City of Casey (location pages).

## Beyond This Keyword Plan — What's Also Needed

- **Google Business Profile:** Service categories, City of Casey service area, Trish's accreditation in description, review strategy. "Near me" searches won through GBP not service pages.
- **Schema markup:** LegalService (type: Attorney) on homepage + service pages. Include name, address, phone, areaServed (City of Casey), services, employee (Trish + LIV credential). FAQ schema where questions are answered. LocalBusiness schema on location pages.
- **NAP consistency:** Match exactly across website, GBP, LIV member directory, True Local, Yellow Pages, local Casey directories.
- **Internal linking:** Homepage → service pages → location pages. Location pages → matching service page. Blog quick wins → relevant service pages.
- **Content production:** 25 pages (5 service + 20 location) need original content built around supporting entities above. Location pages must not be template swaps — suburb-specific content required.
- **Citation building:** LIV member directory, Law Society listings, True Local, Yellow Pages, local Casey directories.
- **Review strategy:** Google reviews impact local pack rankings. wslegal's personal approach is an asset for generating reviews after conveyancing completions.
