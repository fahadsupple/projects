Strategy Notes

Budget Allocation — 1 General = 2 Location
The keyword package is priced at 20 general keyword slots. Location keywords are weighted at 0.5 slots each (1 general = 2 location). Plan 2 uses 17 general keywords (17 slots) + 6 location keywords (3 slots) = exactly 20 slots. Selection criterion: proximity first, volume second. Suburbs within ≤14km from Yagoona are included even at 0 GKP volume — they build local entity signals for the immediate catchment. Suburbs beyond 15km (Parramatta 17km, Hurstville 15km) are excluded regardless of volume. The 16G + 8L alternative (also 20 slots) was not pursued — no 8th close suburb available within budget.
17G × 1 slot + 6L × 0.5 slot = 20 / 20 slots used
How Plan 1 and Plan 2 Work Together
Plan 1 builds category authority — all 11 pages target products at the category and subcategory level. Plan 2 adds geographic authority — the same validated general pages reinforce topical depth while the 7 location pages signal service area and expand Google Business Profile map pack visibility across the Yagoona catchment. Plan 1 establishes what Triple J sells; Plan 2 establishes where they serve. Both are required for a complete local SEO footprint.
Plan 1 = category authority  ·  Plan 2 = geographic authority
General Pages — Selection from Plan 1 Pool
  • 6 lounge subcategory pages (sofas/hub, leather, modular, recliner, fabric, corner) — BDM priority category, highest margin. All SERPs confirmed low competition in Plan 1.
  • Bedroom Furniture — 260/mo, single-keyword page. Broad bedroom hub; no clean secondary available. Adds meaningful volume within budget.
  • Bar Stools — 880/mo primary, +600% YoY. Highest volume in the general pool. Only dining SERP validated clean (all other dining categories returned reject-list nationals at positions 1–4).

Excluded from Plan 2 general: chaise lounge (low volume — 40/mo primary), storage beds (single keyword, 40/mo — lower priority).
Location Pages — Catchment Area Logic
All 7 suburbs sit within 17km of 590 Hume Hwy, Yagoona. For a single-location physical furniture store, customers realistically travel 15–20km maximum. Ranking beyond that radius generates impressions without foot traffic — a worthless ranking for a showroom business. All 44 Sydney suburbs with GKP volume on "furniture store [suburb]" were assessed; only those within the catchment and with clean or amber SERPs were selected. Suburbs outside the radius were excluded regardless of volume or SERP quality.
All suburbs ≤17km from Yagoona · foot traffic is the only conversion goal
GBP + Location Pages — Mutual Reinforcement
Location pages and Google Business Profile reinforce each other. Location pages signal to Google that SW Sydney suburbs are the service area — this expands GBP map pack visibility into those suburbs. GBP authority (reviews, citations) in turn reinforces organic rankings on the location pages. Both workstreams should run in parallel.
