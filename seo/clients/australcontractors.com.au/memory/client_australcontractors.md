## Entity Structure Being Built

**Tier 1 — Homepage:**
"Austral Contractors Group is the Melbourne concrete cutting specialist." Homepage targets "concrete cutting melbourne" — the dominant service identity — consistent with how all major competitors (megasaw, kustomcut, vicsawing) use their homepages. Homepage also anchors Carrum Downs / Dandenong South as the physical location entity.

**Tier 2 — Service pages (topic entities):**
Each service page builds a distinct topic cluster: core drilling, concrete removal, and sawing services (wall + ring sawing). Together they expand Google's understanding: not just "concrete cutting" but specialist capability across drilling, demolition, and sawing techniques. Positions the business as the broadest-capability specialist, not a one-service operator.

**Tier 3 — Location pages (geographic entities):**
24 pages across 12 suburb/regional targets × 2 roots. "Concrete cutting [suburb]" pages = geographic reach across Melbourne metro + regional VIC. "Core drilling [suburb]" pages = specialist drilling authority in each location. Together they tell Google: "This firm operates Melbourne-wide and into regional Victoria."

**Overall entity Google builds:** Concrete cutting specialist (homepage) → specific technique authority (service pages) → geographic coverage across Melbourne + Bendigo/Geelong/Ballarat (location pages).

- **Google Business Profile:** Set up/optimise for primary service (concrete cutting), service area (Melbourne metro + Bendigo), B2B-oriented description, 24/7 emergency response as a differentiator. GBP matters less for B2B trades than B2C, but still relevant for local map visibility.

- **NAP consistency:** Website, GBP, Yellow Pages, hipages, ServiceSeeking, BuildersConnector. Especially important given the two addresses listed (Buontempo Rd + Lord Court) — choose one canonical address.
- **Internal linking:** Homepage → all service pages. Service pages → relevant location pages. Location pages → link back to matching service page. Concrete cutting pages and core drilling pages should cross-reference each other (common to need both services).
- **Content production:** 28 pages (4 service + 24 location) need original content. Concrete removal page especially needs to differentiate Austral's method from generic demolition — this is a known mixed-competitive-set risk. Location pages should reference industrial areas, major projects, and construction activity in each suburb.
- **Citation building:** hipages, ServiceSeeking, BuildersConnector, Yellow Pages, True Local. Civil/construction specific directories if available.
- **B2B trust signals:** Case studies, project photos, client logos (builders/contractors), testimonials from project managers. These are conversion assets — a B2B buyer needs proof of capability before calling.
