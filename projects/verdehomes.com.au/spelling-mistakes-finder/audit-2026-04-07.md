## Brighton page (`/custom-home-builders-brighton/`)

### Issue 1 — Duplicate paragraph (FAQ: KDR vs extension)
Two near-identical sentences appear back-to-back in the same FAQ answer. Old version not removed when new copy was added:
> Para 1: "A knock down rebuild offers you complete design freedom, modern energy effectiveness, and the best possible use of your block. Extensions may compromise existing spaces and often cost similar amounts while delivering less extensive improvements."
> Para 2: "A knock down rebuild gives you complete design freedom, modern energy efficiency, and the best possible use of your block — often for a similar to the final investment as an extension but with far greater results."

**Fix:** Remove Para 1 (old version), keep Para 2 (Google Doc version) — and fix the grammar error in Para 2 (see below).

### Issue 2 — Grammar error (same FAQ answer)
> `"often for a similar to the final investment as an extension"`
Should be: `"often for a similar investment to an extension"` (or similar rework)

### Issue 3 — Missing full stop
> `"Our family-led network of trades and artisans share Verdé's values, ensuring meticulous attention to detail in every element of your home"`
Missing period at end of sentence.

## Rye page (`/custom-home-builders-rye/`)

### Issue 1 — Missing apostrophe
> `"With more than 35 years experience"` → `"35 years' experience"`

### Issue 2 — Sentence fragment with trailing comma
> `"This way, you get to,"` — incomplete sentence before bullet list.
Suggested fix: `"With a Verdé knock down rebuild, you can:"` or remove entirely.

### Issue 3 — Duplicate section content
The "Why Choose Verdé Homes as Your Custom Home Builders in Rye" section repeats the knock down rebuild pitch from the preceding "Expert Knock Down Rebuild Services in Rye" section. Both sections open with essentially the same line ("Love your Rye location but feel your current home no longer suits your lifestyle?") and cover the same KDR benefits. The "Why Choose" section should differentiate with reasons to choose Verdé, not repeat KDR messaging.
