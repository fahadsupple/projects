## Strategic Decisions
- **Conveyancer vs conveyancing:** "Conveyancer" confirmed higher volume at suburb level (Narre Warren: 170 vs 110/mo = 55% gain). Used throughout all location pages and service page.
- **No conveyancer Melbourne service page:** Dropped — 10 Casey suburb pages already cover conveyancing; local pages convert better for conveyancing clients.
- **Clyde North → homepage entity:** Business's home suburb anchored to homepage via NAP/schema — no standalone location page. Replaced by Clyde in location roster.
- **2 roots for location pages:** Root 1 = conveyancer [suburb] (volume), Root 2 = property lawyer [suburb] (brand/positioning — Trish is Accredited Property Law Specialist).
- **Melbourne modifier for service pages:** Kept for wills, probate, business, commercial lease. Dropped for conveyancing.
- **Melbourne modifier for reporting:** Always add Melbourne modifier to secondary keywords even at 0 GKP vol.
