---
name: URL Preference — No City Modifier in Slugs
description: Client prefers clean URLs without city name in slug; put Melbourne in meta title and H1 instead
type: feedback
---

Don't append city names (e.g., "-melbourne") to URL slugs. Keep URLs clean — put the city in the meta title and H1 tag instead.

**Why:** Client preference — confirmed on dantondevelopments.com.au project. Clean slugs, geo-targeting via on-page signals.

**How to apply:** When building URL structures for any client, default to clean slugs (e.g., `/suspended-ceilings/` not `/suspended-ceilings-melbourne/`). Still include the city in meta title, H1, and body copy.
