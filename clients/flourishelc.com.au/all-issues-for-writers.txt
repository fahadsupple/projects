ISSUE 1 — FACTUAL ERROR: "Start Strong Funding" listed on WA pages
============================================================

WHAT IS WRONG:
Start Strong Funding is a New South Wales Government subsidy program.
It does not exist in Western Australia. Mentioning it on WA centre pages
is factually incorrect and may mislead families into thinking they can
access a subsidy that is unavailable in their state.

It is correct on NSW pages only (Oakville, Box Hill, Vineyard, Gables,
Grantham Farm, Riverstone, Mulgrave, Preschool Oakville, Preschool Vineyard).

It must be REMOVED from the following three WA pages:
