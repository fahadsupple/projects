              "We provide end-to-end programs for children from 6 weeks to 5 years..."

  - Page 29 — areas-we-serve
              "We provide extensive care for children from 6 weeks to 6 years across our centres..."

Gables page answer. One of these needs to be corrected for consistency.

     avoids the number entirely and works across both NSW and WA contexts).
