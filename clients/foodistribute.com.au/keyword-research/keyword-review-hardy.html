Mixed Keywords — What to Do

These 6 keywords have partial SERP mismatch. They are not being dropped but need explicit wholesale framing on the page to avoid attracting and bouncing the wrong traffic.

Keyword Vol Issue Guidance
valrhona chocolate australia 260 Amazon and consumer cake shops in top results. Keep. Page must lead with "wholesale" and "trade pricing" — include minimum order quantities and B2B application. Do not frame as a consumer product page.
la conviette butter 260 Specialty consumer retailers alongside wholesale (Eustralis). Keep. Wholesale intent is present and Foodistribute is already a known distributor for this product. Frame around trade quantities and restaurant applications.
wholesale parmesan cheese 10 Woolworths and Harris Farm appear in results. Keep. The "wholesale" modifier is doing most of the filtering work. Explicit B2B copy (case quantities, trade account, minimum order) will help. Low volume — acceptable risk.
asian food wholesalers 2,900 SERP dominated by Asian food specialists (Ettason, Rockman, BKK). Foodistribute would be competing as a generalist against category specialists. Keep only if Foodistribute has a substantial, well-stocked Asian food category (Japanese pantry, pan-Asian sauces, etc.). If the range is thin, visitors from this keyword will leave immediately. Confirm with client before building the page.
asian food wholesalers australia 10 Same issue as #23 — specialist-dominated SERP. Same guidance as above. Low volume makes the risk lower — works fine as a secondary keyword on an Asian food hub page.
hospitality food suppliers 20 "Hospitality" modifier pulls in equipment and fit-out suppliers. Keep as a secondary keyword on the foodservice page — not a standalone page. Pairing it with a clean primary like "foodservice distributors australia" (#33) anchors the page intent correctly.
