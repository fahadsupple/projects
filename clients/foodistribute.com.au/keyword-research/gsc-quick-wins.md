2. **Product page CTR is broken**: palm sugar (5,621 impr, 0.02% CTR), lurpak (3,451 impr, 0.03% CTR), beloka (2,831 impr, 0% CTR). These products rank well but the title tags are likely bare product names with no purchase intent signal. Fix: add "Buy Wholesale", "Trade Price", or "Food Service" to title tags.
