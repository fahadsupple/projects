## What information we collect

Depending on the nature of your matter, we may collect:

- Your name, date of birth, and contact details (address, phone, email)
- Identity documents required for Verification of Identity
- Financial information relevant to your matter — such as a property purchase price or payment details
- Details about the legal matter you've engaged us for (property, estate, business)
- Sensitive information where relevant and with your consent — for example, health information in the context of medical powers of attorney or estate planning
- Information you submit through our website contact or enquiry form

We only collect information that's directly necessary. If it's not relevant to helping you, we won't ask for it.

---

## How we collect it

We collect your information:

- Directly from you via phone, email, or our website enquiry form
- During the course of your matter, as your instructions evolve
- From third parties involved in your matter — other solicitors, real estate agents, banks, government bodies — where necessary to carry out the work
- Via Docusign when you sign engagement documents electronically

---

## Why we need it

We use your personal information to:

- Open and manage your matter file
- Provide you with legal advice and services
- Communicate with you and any relevant third parties throughout your matter
- Meet our obligations under the Legal Profession Uniform Law (Victoria) and other applicable legislation
- Complete your Verification of Identity as required by law
- Issue invoices and process payments
- Respond to your enquiries
- Comply with anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing laws
- Improve the quality of our services

---

## Who we share it with

We may share your personal information with:

- Third parties directly involved in your matter — other solicitors, real estate agents, Titles Victoria, ASIC, the Supreme Court of Victoria, banks, and relevant government bodies
- Service providers who assist us in operating our practice (document management, accounting software, Docusign) — only to the extent necessary and under confidentiality obligations
- Our professional indemnity insurer, where relevant
- A regulatory body if required by law

We will never sell your personal information. We won't share it for marketing purposes. We won't send it overseas without your consent unless the law requires it.

## How we keep it secure

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, and disclosure. That includes secure file management systems, password-protected software, and physical security at our Clyde North office.

One honest note: no data transmission over the internet is completely secure. If you contact us via our website form or email, please be aware that online transmission carries some inherent risk.
