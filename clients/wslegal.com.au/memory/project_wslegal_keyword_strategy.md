## Budget
- 40 location-equivalent units
- 10 General keywords (×2 units = 20) + 20 Location keywords (×1 unit = 20)
- Split: 10G + 20L — BDM's recommended split

## Key Strategic Decisions

- **Conveyancer vs conveyancing:** "Conveyancer" confirmed higher volume at suburb level (Narre Warren: 170 vs 110 = 55% gain). Used throughout all location pages and service page.
- **No conveyancer Melbourne service page:** Dropped — 10 Casey suburb pages already cover conveyancing; local pages convert better for conveyancing clients; starting from zero commercial rankings.
- **Clyde North → homepage entity:** Business's home suburb anchored to homepage via NAP/schema — no standalone location page. Replaced by Clyde in location roster.
- **2 roots for location pages:** Root 1 = conveyancer [suburb] (volume), Root 2 = property lawyer [suburb] (brand/positioning — Trish is Accredited Property Law Specialist).
- **Melbourne modifier for service pages:** Kept for wills, probate, business, commercial lease — these services are genuinely accessible remotely. Dropped for conveyancing (local conversion issue).
- **Melbourne modifier for reporting:** Always add Melbourne modifier to secondary keywords even at 0 GKP vol — easier to track and present rankings to client.

## SERP Key Learnings
1. "conveyancing melbourne" SERP dominated by specialist conveyancers, NOT law firms — conveyancer Melbourne page dropped; location pages handle conveyancing
2. "powers of attorney melbourne" SERP = government/informational — not targetable commercially
3. "probate melbourne" SERP mixed (government + informational + commercial) — replaced with "deceased estate lawyer melbourne" (50/mo, clean law-firm SERP)
4. Accredited Property Law Specialist angle: Davis Lawyers and Elamine Lawyers are direct competitors
5. "commercial lease lawyer melbourne" = only 70/mo but $26+ CPC — high per-matter value justifies inclusion
6. Location pages for "property lawyer [suburb]": Berwick has most competition; Hampton Park/Beaconsfield/Officer/Hallam/Endeavour Hills have minimal law firm presence
7. Duffy & Simon traffic = mostly family law — NOT direct competitor for wslegal's services
