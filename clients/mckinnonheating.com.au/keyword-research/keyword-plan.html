Business Profile

McKinnon Heating & Cooling is a privately owned Melbourne HVAC specialist established in 1973. Headquartered in Moorabbin, the business is co-owned by Craig Gambrell, Simon Wilding, and Scott Handley — a collective industry experience of 100+ years. Preferred provider for Peninsula, Bayside, and inner-city builders. Google rating 4.7/5.

Content Gaps & Priority Actions

Critical Content Gaps

CRITICAL — No Suburb Landing Pages (Zero of 50 suburbs covered)

The single biggest SEO gap on mckinnonheating.com.au. Competitors with suburb pages consistently outrank McKinnon for local queries even when they have lower domain authority. Every target suburb has a direct organic ranking opportunity that currently doesn't exist.

Action: Create 50 suburb service pages — one per target suburb. Each page targets one primary keyword with suburb-specific content, proximity signals, and builder partnership mentions where applicable. Expected to drive 40+ page-one location rankings within 6-12 months.

HIGH — Hydronic Heating Page Depth

1,000 monthly searches, KD 22, no HVAC competitor in top 5. Current site likely has thin or generic hydronic content. Enormous untapped opportunity in the premium Bayside/inner-south heritage home market.

Action: Build dedicated /hydronic-heating page with system types, Victorian home suitability, Bayside installation case studies, cost guide, comparison to ducted, and VEU heat pump eligibility information.

HIGH — Split System Page Depth

590/mo with KD 25. SK AIR at #3 has DA 18 and a thin page — directly displaceable. McKinnon's wall split + multi-head capability is undersold in the current content structure.

Action: Expand /split-systems: wall split vs multi-head differentiation, brand range (Daikin, Mitsubishi, Fujitsu), supply + install offering, licensing credentials, suburb service list.

MEDIUM — Gas-to-Electric Conversion Content

McKinnon claims industry leadership in gas-to-electric conversions but this is not reflected in any current SEO content. Growing category as VIC gas phase-out gains momentum. VEU program participation is a direct commercial advantage.

Action: Create VEU program landing page + gas-to-electric conversion guide targeting 'gas to electric heating melbourne' and related terms.
