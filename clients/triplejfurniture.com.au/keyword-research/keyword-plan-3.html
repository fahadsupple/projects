SERP Reasoning — National Strategy at DR 8

Strategic Framework — Why Sub-Categories
At DR 8, top-level furniture category SERPs nationally are chain-dominated and unwinnable in the short term. The primary national chains — Harvey Norman, IKEA, Freedom, Amart, Nick Scali, Fantastic Furniture, Plush, Domayne, T&W, La-Z-Boy, Koala Living — hold positions 1–4 on most high-volume category terms. The strategy: target material- and style-specific sub-categories that chains don't have dedicated collection pages for. These sub-category SERPs return boutique specialists, interior retailers, and smaller ecommerce stores — a competitive set Triple J Furniture can rank alongside.

Chain rejection threshold: Chains at positions 1–4 = REJECT. Chains at positions 5–8 = AMBER (viable with content quality). Chains at 9+ = clean enough.
