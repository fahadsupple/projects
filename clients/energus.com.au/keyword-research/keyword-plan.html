Keyword Reasoning

Budget structure — 10 general + 20 location

Standard package is 10 general + 20 location pages. General budget includes all city-level pages — Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Adelaide each consume one general slot (4 total), leaving 6 slots for national service pages. Location pages are suburb/regional city level only — not capital cities.

For Energus, suburb-level targeting has no search volume for commercial solar (B2B buyer doesn't search "commercial solar Bondi" — they search "commercial solar sydney" or "commercial solar company"). The location layer is therefore filled with major industrial and regional cities across the 4 target states, where commercial solar ROI is a credible pitch even at 0 search volume (industrial energy costs drive demand, not keyword awareness).

The /commercial-solar/ hub page doesn't currently exist. This is the core brand anchor — the page a buyer lands on when they want to understand who Energus is and what they do. "Commercial solar company" (260/mo) is navigational-intent: the buyer has decided they want commercial solar and is now selecting a company. Create new page at /commercial-solar/ — highest structural priority
Industrial solar — URL fix required

The existing page at /industrial_solar_energy/ uses underscores — Google treats underscores as word joiners, not separators, meaning "industrial_solar_energy" is read as a single token, not "industrial solar energy." This is a known ranking disadvantage. The new URL /industrial-solar/ is shorter, hyphen-separated, and matches the target keyword pattern. Create the new page, migrate content, and 301 the old URL.

Fix underscore URL before optimising content
Location page strategy — industrial cities, not suburbs

Commercial solar is a B2B product — buyers are businesses, not households. Suburb-level targeting (e.g., "commercial solar Parramatta") has effectively zero search volume. The location layer for Energus uses major regional and industrial cities where commercial and industrial energy usage is high:

  • QLD (9 pages): Gold Coast (90/mo), Sunshine Coast (70/mo), Townsville, Cairns, Mackay, Toowoomba, Bundaberg, Gladstone (LNG + Rio Tinto aluminium), Ipswich, Rockhampton
  • NSW (5 pages): Newcastle (Hunter Valley), Central Coast, Penrith (Western Sydney logistics), Wollongong (BlueScope Steel), Albury (Hume Highway logistics)
  • VIC (3 pages): Geelong (Port, Ford/Alcoa legacy), Ballarat, Bendigo
  • SA (2 pages): Whyalla (GFG Steel), Port Augusta (energy transition hub)

Zero-volume pages are included where industrial energy intensity makes commercial solar ROI compelling regardless of search demand — they also build geographic authority signals for Energus's multi-state operation.
