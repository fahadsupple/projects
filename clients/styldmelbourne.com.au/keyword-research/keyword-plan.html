Implementation Notes

Key technical and content considerations for the WIX site build.

Build Priority Order

Build and publish in this order to maximise early ranking impact:

Priority Pages Rationale
Week 1–2 Homepage, Property Styling, Pricing, Packages, About Us (rebuild) Core commercial pages — these generate revenue. All have confirmed search volume.
Week 3–4 Full Styling, Partial Styling, Vacant, Occupied, Real Estate Agents Service differentiation + B2B audience. Supports conversion from homepage.

Month 2–3 Artwork, Outdoor, Furniture, Is Home Staging Worth It?, Comparison, Preparation Guide Topical authority build-out and informational content for PAA targeting.

URL Structure Rule

"Melbourne" does not appear in URL slugs. City modifier goes in the meta title and H1 instead. Clean slugs: /property-styling/ not /property-styling-melbourne/. This keeps URLs shorter and avoids keyword-stuffed paths. WIX handles URL customisation via Page Settings → URL slug.

Review Signals

The local pack for both main terms is dominated by businesses with 50–298 Google reviews. STYLD has 0 public reviews visible. Prioritise Google Business Profile setup and review collection from the 42 completed jobs immediately. Target: 20+ reviews within 60 days of GBP going live.

Content Depth Requirement

Competing sites (stagedhomes.com.au, propertystylingmelbourne.com.au) have substantial content on their core pages. Minimum 600 words per Tier 1–2 page. Each page must include: clear service description, who it's for, process overview, FAQ (minimum 3 questions), and a free quote CTA.
