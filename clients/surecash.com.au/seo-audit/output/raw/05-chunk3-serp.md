**Impact:** PAA slots are click magnets — capturing even 1 PAA position for "payday loans" (pos ~22 overall) would generate substantial traffic.
**Fix:** Implement FAQPage schema on relevant service pages. Create FAQ content targeting confirmed PAA questions: "What is a payday loan in Australia?", "Are payday loans legal in Australia?", "How quickly can I get a cash loan?", "Can I get a cash loan on Centrelink?"
**Effort:** Medium (2-3 days content + schema implementation)
**Cross-reference:** See Phase 1 for schema implementation issues.

### INTENT-002 — [HIGH] Heavy Paid Ad Presence Compressing Organic Visibility
**Evidence:** 4-6 paid ads appear above organic results for payday loans, cash loans, fast cash loans, quick cash loans. This pushes organic position 1 to effectively the 7th visible result on the page.
**Impact:** Even if surecash.com.au achieves position 5 organically, visible placement would be below the fold due to paid ad density.
**Fix:** Content strategy must target featured snippets and PAA positions to appear above the paid ads zone. Long-form informational content clusters needed.
**Effort:** High (ongoing content program)

### KW-006 — [HIGH] Local Pack Presence ≠ Organic Visibility — Wrong Metric Being Tracked
**Evidence:** SE Ranking tracks local pack positions as primary metric. All 5 confirmed Tier 1 keywords show organic positions of 22-37 in GSC — none in top 10.
**Impact:** Reporting bias — local pack positions give false confidence in keyword performance. If GBP ranking drops or pack competition changes, zero organic safety net exists.
**Fix:** Separate tracking: configure SE Ranking to distinguish local pack vs organic. Set up organic-only tracking for the 10 Tier 1 keywords.
**Effort:** Low (1 hour SE Ranking configuration)
