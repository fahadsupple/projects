## Key Strategic Decisions

1. **Single location silo (rendering supplies):** More focused than splitting across waterproofing/tiling/cladding — renderers are the largest trade audience; official Rockcote+Unitex distributor status creates genuine content authority on every location page
2. **New domain strategy:** KD 4-8 location pages are the fastest ranking path. Brand+distributor pages (KD 8-19) follow. Generic head terms (building supplies KD 34) are long-term anchors only
3. **Bowens/National Tiles avoidance:** No generic terms where these chains dominate multiple positions. Metrobuild's trade specialisation is the positioning angle.
4. **G07 + G14 share /tiling-supplies/:** KD difference justifies a single page — G07 (KD 14, adhesives supplier) primary, G14 (KD 22, tiling supplies) secondary
5. **Richmond in location grid:** Inner-city commercial page targets boutique builders and fitout contractors — audience for venetian plaster and micro cement cross-sell
6. **Decorative finishes (venetian plaster + micro cement):** Niche, high-CPC ($4.20-$5.10), low-KD (9-13) — best risk-adjusted pages in the portfolio. No named competitor has pages for these products.
7. **G15 (hebel vs fibre cement cladding):** Only informational-commercial page (within 15% budget rule). Featured snippet opportunity from hipages (DA 58) — capturable by a genuine distributor of both products.
