Key Findings from Research
  • No dominant brand-specific distributor exists in Melbourne's rendering/cladding space. Bell Plaster (#3 for rendering supplies) has no Hebel, no James Hardie, no waterproofing. This gap is the entire strategy.
  • Rockcote, Unitex, NRG Greenboard, and James Hardie brand+supplier queries are ALL KD 8–19. Official distributor status is a decisive ranking signal for these — DA 18–28 competitors (Crowntex, Archiway) can be beaten.
  • SERP for micro cement and venetian plaster (Melbourne) are almost empty. Micro cement: #1 is DA 12. Venetian plaster: top 3 are DA 14–18. First-mover wins available within 60–90 days of launch.
  • Suburb-level rendering supply queries are KD 4–5 with directories only in top 3. These are the fastest wins for a new domain.
  • Bowens and National Tiles are NOT threats on brand+distributor or suburb-level terms. They dominate broad category terms (building supplies, tiling supplies) but do not compete on Rockcote/Unitex/Hebel-specific searches.
  • PAA data reveals high-value intent signals: "Does Bowens sell Hebel panels?" (confirms users know Bowens is NOT the source), "Who are authorised James Hardie suppliers in Melbourne?" (directly answers to Metrobuild's landing page).

Keyword Strategy

Strategy Rationale: New Domain + Official Distributor = Brand+Suburb Focus

Why we avoided generic head terms

"Building supplies melbourne" (390/mo, KD 34) has Bunnings at #1 (DA 78) and Bowens at #2 (DA 52). A new domain at DA 4 cannot compete within a 12-month window. Including it on the homepage as a long-term anchor is correct strategy — but it is not a 12-month ranking expectation.

Similarly, "tiling supplies melbourne" (140/mo, KD 22) is dominated by National Tiles (DA 48) — but the trade-specific variant "tiling adhesives supplier melbourne" (KD 14, Archiway at #3 with DA 22) is directly winnable.

Every keyword selected was tested against: Can Metrobuild realistically outrank the current top-3 within 12 months given DA 4 starting point?

New domain strategy: win on KD 4–19 brand+supplier and suburb terms first, build authority for broader terms later
Why official distributor status is the core positioning asset

For brand+supplier queries ("rockcote rendering systems melbourne", "unitex render system melbourne", "hebel panels supplier melbourne", "james hardie cladding supplier melbourne"), Google rewards official distributor pages specifically because users searching these terms have brand loyalty already — they want to find a local authorised source.

Metrobuild is officially authorised for 9 brands. No competitor holds this breadth of official status. Rockcote's own site + Metrobuild as distributor = the expected 1-2 pattern. Crowntex (DA 18) currently at #3 for Rockcote is not a formidable blocker.

9 brand distributor pages = 9 low-KD ranking opportunities that no competitor can replicate without the same authorisations
How the 15 general keywords are allocated

Tier A — 8 keywords (must-have): Homepage hub + rendering silo + brand distributor pages for the highest-CPC brands (James Hardie, Hebel, waterproofing, tiling). These are the core revenue pages.

Tier B — 4 keywords: NRG Greenboard (KD 8, near-zero competition), external cladding systems hub (comparison page), venetian plaster (KD 13, no named competitor has content), tiling supplies secondary.

Tier C — 3 keywords: Micro cement (KD 9, highest CPC at $5.10, first-mover), wall sarking (KD 7, purchase-ready niche), hebel vs fibre cement cladding (comparison content pillar, only informational keyword).

Why a single location silo (rendering supplies)

With 10 location slots, splitting across multiple service silos would produce thin, low-quality location pages. A single silo at "rendering supplies" concentrates authority on the client's largest trade audience (renderers/plasterers), allows genuine content depth on each page (Rockcote/Unitex distribution + delivery to that suburb), and gives the best ranking velocity for a new domain.

Springvale is the store's home suburb and maps to the homepage — no standalone Springvale location page is needed or created.

Rendering supplies silo: highest trade volume, official distributor content, KD 4–5 = fastest wins for a new domain
B2B guardrails applied

Every keyword was screened for SERP intent contamination. Terms where Bunnings, Masters, DIY content, or homeowner guides dominate were excluded regardless of volume. This is a trade store — appearing in consumer SERPs drives irrelevant traffic with near-zero conversion.

Excluded: cement render (broad consumer SERP), render a wall (DIY SERP), acrylic texture (Bunnings SERP), PPE (no B2B trade SERP)
